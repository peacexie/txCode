
<b>Words About Love爱情蜜语</b>

◇ To the world you may be one person, but to one person you may be the world.
(对于世界而言，你是一个人 ；但是对于某个人，你是其整个世界。)

◇ Love me little, love me long.
(爱不贵亲密，而贵长久。)

◇ Love makes man grow up or sink down.
(爱情，要么让人成熟，要么让人堕落。)

◇ The key for happiness is not to fi nd a perfect person, but fi nd someone and build a perfect relationship with him.
(幸福的关键不在于找到一个完美的人，而在找到一个人，然后和他一起努力建立一个完美的关系。)

◇ Never frown, even when you are sad, because you never know who is falling in love with your smile.
(纵然伤心，也不要愁眉不展，因为你不知是谁会爱上你的笑容。)

◇ I love you not because of who you are, but because of who I am when I am with you.
(我爱你，不是因为你是一个怎样的人，而是因为我喜欢与你在一起时的感觉。)
